SINGAPORE POLYTECHNIC'S EFFORTS

Singapore Polytechnic has made efforts in terms of environmental sustainability

SP ENVIRONMENT CLUB

The SP Environment Club is a CCA group that is concerned about the natural environment and is commited to promoting a sustainable lifestyle on campus and beyond.

You can find more information about them on their instagram page @sp_env


GREEN MARK CETIFICATION

SP campus buildings were designed and constructed with environmentally friendly features and sustainable practices, with the aim to certify all 22 buildings by 2025.

Because of this, SP has been awarded the Green Mark certification from the BCA (Building and Construction Authority).

WATER MANAGEMENT

Singapore Polytechnic has installed water dispensers around the campus. They are placed in various locations to provide convenient access to drinking water for students, staff and visitors. SP has also minimised the use of single-use plastic bottles.

SOLAR ENERGY

SP has installed solar panels across the campus, using solar energy for energy conservation. This helps to reduce the need for conventional energy and reduces the greenhosue gas emissions.


WASTE MANAGEMENT

Recycling bins have been placed everywhere around the campus, and recycling activies have been actively encouraged among students, staff and visitors.

ENVIRONEMNTAL EDUCATION

Singapore Polytechinc has impleneted environemntal education into it's curriculum , with the goal to equip students with knowledge on environmental sustainability.
