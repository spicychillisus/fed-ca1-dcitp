SAVING THE EARTH

My personal opinion on what we can do

There are so much that we can do to help in saving our earth. But first, let's go over the facts.

The earth's climate changes everyday due to burning of fossil fuels, deforestation and other industrial processes. Fossil fuels are burned in order to create energy for vehicles to run, deforestation happens to clear spaces for developments by burning down the trees, producing smoke, and industrial processes pollute the air. Such activities can lead to temperatures increasing, melting glaciers, sea-level rise, and more frequent natural disasters such as floods and draughts. This is what is known as Climate Change.

My personal opinion on what we can do in saving the earth is that we can try to educate others on the importance of sustainability and how they can help to create a positive impact on the environment. By doing so, more people will be educated on the importance of sustainability on earth and more measures can be taken to reduce the risk of climate change and make our earth a much better place to live in.

Other Measures

A YouTuber by the name of MrBeast started initiatives to help in saving the earth, named #TEAMTREES and #TEAMSEAS.

The #TEAMTREES campaign was started in 2019. The goal of the campaign was to raise funds to plant 20 million trees by the end of 2019. It gained widespread attention and support from the online community. Donations were made through the #TEAMTREES website, with every dollar contributing to planting one tree. The campaign successfully reached it's target by the end of 2019.

With the success of #TEAMTREES, another campaign was started, named #TEAMSEAS. The goal was to remove 30 million pounds of platic and debris from the world's oceans. MrBeast partnered with The Ocean Cleanup, a non-profit organisation dedicated to developing technologies to removing plastic waste from the oceans. The campaign also aimed to raise funds through donations to support cleanup efforts. It received worldwide support, and funds raised were used to develop technologies for effective ocean cleaup.

MY PLANS

So, what are my plans to help in saving the earth?

I have 3 personal goals that I aspire to achieve to help save the earth.

Goal 1 Goal 2 Goal 3

Goal 1: Reducing Waste

My first personal goal is to reduce the amount of waste I produce. I plan to use reusable containers and water bottles, as well as avoid using single-use plastics like straws and bags, compost food waste, and recycle properly. To do so, I will buy products made from sustainable materials, such as recycled paper or bamboo, to reduce waste in the long term.

Goal 2: Using sustainable transportation

Although this is something that many of us do on a daily basis, mainly because it's part of the norm, my next personal goal is to use sustainable transportation, which means that instead of using vehicles that run on diesel, I will try and opt for eco-friendly vehicles that run on clean natural gas or electricity. These kind of vehicles don't pollute the environment as they are much more cleaner to run.

Goal 3: Reuse, Reduce, Recycle

The 3Rs, Reuse, Reduce and Recycle promote sustainable practices that reduces the environmental impact in our daily lives.

Reduce refers to reducing the amount of waste that we create. We can do so by reducing the amount of resources that we use by buying what we need and avoiding single-use items.

Reuse refers to using products or materials that have already been used and using them again instead of disposing them away after single use. I'm already starting to do this but I will reuse plastic shopping bags from NTUC instead of throwing them away and reuse them for other uses such as carrying spare clothing.

Recycle refers to converting old waste materials into new products, reducing the need to extract raw materials to make new materials. Items like paper, and plastic can all be recycled to form new products.

Using the 3Rs, it will help to create a more sustainable earth to live in for all.
